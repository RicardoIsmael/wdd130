Overview

Purpose

we want to use the powerful tool that is the internet to show our services and share with people and families the fantastic rafting experience and contact with nature.

Audience

Target customers would be tourists, families, youth, and scholar groups. each group would have services and rafting pathways specific according to age, and knowledge about rafting. The page would show every level of rafting difficulty. There will be a course for rafting beginners for those people that don't have experience. For people that don't like water, we have wonderful hiking around of mountains.

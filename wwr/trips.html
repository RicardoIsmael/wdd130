Mountain-Trip

Enjoy the beautiful mountain and lakes.

On this trip for three days, we will be in some of the most beautiful places in Cataratas Falls. we'll camp near the falls called two sisters which have an amazing vistage of the lakes. we'll walk through the forest feeling the peace of nature, the creations of God. we'll sight animals and birds in their natural environment.

Join Us!
Lake-Trip

Knowing the lakes.

On this trip of four days, we'll go through five beautiful lakes. we'll camp and we'll fish one of the most delicious fishes, the dorado fish. We'll teach you to cook it! we'll visit deep caverns with amazing histories of cave painting. we'll climb the ombu mountain and we'll sight the seven falls of Cataratas, we'll feel very close to the sky.

Join Us!
River-Trip

Traveling through the river

On this trip of three days, we'll enjoy rafting through fifteen miles of the wild river. We recommend if you don't have experience with rafting take another option of a trip. we'll travel for one of the most turbulent currents of the Parana river. We'll camp on the river shore. We'll enjoy the quiet and peace of the night. We'll sight a common animal of the river the yacare, some kind of crocodile. And much more.

Join Us!
